Frequently Asked Questions

Contract farming is an agricultural arrangement where a farmer agrees to produce a certain crop, livestock, or product based on an agreement with a buyer.

Farmers benefit from guaranteed market access, reduced financial risk

Buyers can ensure a consistent supply of agricultural products at a fair agreed price

Fair pricing can be ensured through transparent negotiations, understanding market trends

Our History

We realized that farmers, the backbone of our society, needed a platform where they didn’t just grow crops but grew opportunities, security, and prosperity. Buyers, on the other hand, wanted consistency, quality, and reliability, especially in a world where supply chains were often fragile. What if we could marry those two needs into one seamless digital experience? “What if we could build a bridge between farmers and buyers? A platform where they could meet, trust, and collaborate? And what if we could guarantee them both stability and reliability?” That was the eureka moment. Contract farming — the perfect solution! It was an age-old practice but needed a modern, digital twist to really make it work for today's farmers and businesses.
